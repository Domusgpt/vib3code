HOW TO PLAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. 🚀 START THE GAME
   • Click the "START GAME" button to begin playing
   • The game area will become active and ready for interaction
   • Your cursor will change to indicate interactive mode

2. 🎯 PLACE BLOCKS
   • Click anywhere within the dark game canvas
   • Each click creates a colorful block at that location
   • Blocks appear with satisfying animations and glowing effects
   • Try to create patterns, structures, or artistic arrangements

3. 📈 SCORE POINTS
   • Each block placement earns you 10 points
   • Your score updates in real-time in the stats display
   • Higher scores unlock new visual effects and animations
   • Compete with yourself to achieve new high scores

4. 🆙 LEVEL PROGRESSION
   • Every 100 points advances you to the next level
   • Level ups trigger special celebratory animations
   • Higher levels introduce new color palettes
   • Watch for surprise visual enhancements as you progress

5. ⏸️ PAUSE & RESET
   • Click "PAUSE GAME" to temporarily stop interaction
   • Use "RESET" to clear all blocks and start fresh
   • Your progress is saved during pause mode
   • Reset allows you to try new creative approaches
